Why Fetchabl?

We believe every dog deserves a chance to be seen - and every adopter deserves a better way to find them. But with over 1,000 rescue organisations in the UK, amazing dogs often go unnoticed, hidden behind outdated websites and scattered systems. Fetchabl is here to fix that. We're building a clean, modern platform that makes it easy to discover adoptable dogs, and gives rescue centres the visibility and tools they need to rehome more dogs, faster. No more chasing down rabbit holes. Just a smarter, friendlier way to adopt.
