Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

At Fetchabl, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website (fetchabl.com).

1. Who We Are

Fetchabl is a UK-based platform helping people discover rescue dogs from local shelters. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@fetchabl.com.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site. This includes anonymised data like pages visited, time on site, and browser type.
  • Email Signups: If you subscribe to our waitlist or newsletter, we collect your email address.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To send occasional updates (if you've subscribed)
  • To understand usage trends and improve features

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for analytics purposes via Google Analytics. You will be asked for your consent before any non-essential cookies are used. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletions
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@fetchabl.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be posted here.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, reach out at hello@fetchabl.com.
